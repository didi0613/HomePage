GoToTraining
Citrix Systems Inc July 2014
GoToTraining is a web-conferencing tool for hosting online classes and trainings. Hands up and pencil down is the simple answer to online learning.
GoToTraining Instant Join & Breakout
GoToTraining is an app allows people to attend real-time, virtual classes that can incorporate audio, video, and screen sharing of any application or website. People can review recorded lectures and demonstrations created by their instructors. Moreover, they can also meet and collaborate with their instructors and classmates during the instant join and breakout sessions.
