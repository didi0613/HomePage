GoToTraining is a web-conferencing tool for hosting online classes and trainings.
GoToTraining Instant Join & Breakout
    GoToTraining is an app allows people to start the meeting in seconds. Without the GoToMeeting installed, we also support the people joins the meeting from our web app. Once attendees click the join link or enter with the meeting ID, they join the meeting and immediately join the meeting with the audio options.
