GoToMeeting Instant Join
GoToMeeting is an app allows people to start the meeting in seconds. Without the GoToMeeting installed, we also support people join the meeting from our web app. Once attendees click the join link or enter with the meeting ID, they can join the meeting immediately with audio options.
